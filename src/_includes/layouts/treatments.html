Denplan {% include "partials/_text-denplan.svg" %}

Denplan is Cymru / Wales’ leading dental payment plan. It was designed to spread the cost of treatments, to make looking after your dental health easy and affordable. Denplan’s supported by more than 6,600 dentists nationwide and helps care for around 1.4 million patients. Denplan Essentials gives you routine preventive care which includes:

  • Check-ups
  • Scaling, polishing, and other hygiene treatments
  • Preventive dental advice
  • Dental x-rays
  • Any additional treatments specified by the practice
