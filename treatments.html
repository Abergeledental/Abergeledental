Denplan

Denplan is Cymru / Wales’ leading dental payment plan. It was designed to spread the cost of treatments, to make looking after your dental health easy and affordable. Denplan’s supported by more than 6,600 dentists nationwide and helps care for around 1.4 million patients. The benefits of Denplan include oral health advice, regular check-ups, and set payments.
